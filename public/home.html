How to Play Pusoy Dos

What is Pusoy Dos?

Pusoy Dos is a turn based card game played with up 4 players (each dealt 13 cards) with the object to drop all of your cards first. The Card Ranking also known as the Card Order determines what set of cards can defeat another.

How to Win

The goal of Pusoy Dos is to drop all 13 of your cards first.

 

Card Order

The cards are the same types of hands as found in poker, as in the combinations of cards listed below:

Singles

The lowest single the 3 of club’s.

The highest single is 2 of diamond’s.

Doubles (Pairs a.k.a. two-of-a-kind)

The lowest double is a pair 3’s.

Triples (three-of-a-kind)

The lowest triple of 333’s

 

 

The highest three of a kind is of 222’s

 

 

Five Cards

Straight

A straight is a five card hand made up of 5 consecutive single cards (i.e. 3,4,5,6,7) The ranking goes off the highest card in the straight.

The lowest straight is 2,3,4,5,6

 

 

The highest straight is 10,J,Q,K,A

 

 

What if two players both have a straight with the same highest card such a straight ending in 7’s, which straight is higher?

Answer: The higher straight would be the straight with the high card with the higher suit so a 7clubs high straight is lower than a 7diamonds high straight.

Flush

The lowest flush is a club flush which can me made with any 5 cards of the same suit.

For ex: 3-clubs, Q-clubs, 7-clubs, K-clubs, 5-clubs

 

 

The highest flush is a diamond flush.

Q: If another player has a flush of the same suit, which flush would be higher?

Player 1: Ace of hearts, 3-hearts, 8-hearts, 7-hearts, 9-hearts

Player 2: 2 of hearts, King of hearts, 6-hearts, 4-hearts, 10-hearts

Answer: Player 2, when two flushes are of the same suit, the higher of the flushes would be determined by the highest single card within that hand i.e. a king high flush is higher than a jack high flush.

Full House

A full house is a five card hand made up of three-of-a-kind and a pair i.e. 3,3,3,J,J or etc. The ranking goes off the triple (three-of-a-kind) so a full house of 3’s would be lower than a full house of 10’s. The lowest full house is a full house of 333’s and the highest full house is a full house of 222’s

 

 

Quads (Four-of-a-kind)

A quad is four of a kind and a kicker or any card you choose (i.e. K,K,K,K,3)

The lowest four of a kind is flush is 3333, and any single card. (i.e. 3,3,3,3,J)

The highest four of a kind is 2222, and any single card. (i.e. 2,2,2,2,5)

 

 

Straight Flush

The lowest straight flush is 2,3,4,5,6,7

The highest straight flush is A,K,Q,J,10

The highest straight flush is a diamond flush.

 

 

Q: If another player has a straight flush of the same suit, which flush would be higher?

Player 1: Ace of hearts, King of hearts, Queen of hearts, Jack of hearts, 10 of hearts

Player 2: 9 of hearts, 8 of hearts, 7 of hearts, 6 of hearts, 5 of hearts

Answer: Player 1, when two flushes are of the same suit-  even Straight flushes, the higher of the flushes would be determined by the highest single card within that hand i.e. Ace high flush is higher than a jack high flush. A Royal Flush is a A,K,Q,J,10 straight flush.

Rounds

Who goes first?

The lowest card goes first which is the 3 of clubs. However the 3 of clubs can be played within a combination of cards i.e. as a single, within a pair of 3’s, as a triple 333’s, or within any five card hand.

Play or Pass

The Rotation

The rotation of turns as in poker goes clockwise (the player to your immediate left would play their cards after yours.

To Drop a Card(s)

In order to drop a card during your turn, the card combination must be higher than the one currently in play (the card combination played before you), so if you played a single 3, I’d have to play a higher single if I wanted to drop a card.

Passing

If you don’t have any card higher than the one in play or if you just want to save a card for another turn, you can pass. When you pass the turn transfers to the next player to your left. Passing only applies for the duration of that card. So if Idropped a 3 of clubs, you dropped King of diamonds, player 3 passed, and if player 4 beats it with a Ace of clubs. I could drop a 2 of spades.

What happens if everyone passes on my card?

You now have control. You can drop any combination of cards that you like.
